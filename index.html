Our Mission

In a world that sells your data, we give you Control.

Today, "Cloud" has become synonymous with surveillance. Every document uploaded to an external server is a piece of your identity that no longer fully belongs to you.

Our purpose is to prove that you can have cutting-edge processing power without sacrificing a single pixel of privacy. Your processor is the only cloud you need.
