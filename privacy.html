Privacy Manifesto

Effective Date: April 2026

01. Radical Local Processing

At VoltScan, privacy isn't a setting—it's the architecture. Your documents never touch our servers because **we don't have servers.**

On-Device OCR

Text recognition happens using your phone's neural engine. No data leaves the device.

Zero Observation

Since files are local, no one—not even the developers—can see what you scan.

02. What data do we collect?

Since we have **no user accounts**, our data footprint is nearly zero. We only interact with:

  • No Email / No Phone Numbers
  • No Document Metadata Collection
  • Anonymous Analytics: Only crash reports and basic usage patterns to improve performance (e.g., "how many times was the signature tool used").

03. Payment & Billing Privacy

Subscriptions are processed through the official app stores. VoltScan does not possess your financial identity.

Transaction Method App Store / Play Store
Card Data Stored Never

Billing is tied to your Apple ID or Google Account. We only receive a confirmation token to activate your features.

04. Device Permissions

To function, VoltScan requires access to:

  • Camera: For document capture only. The feed is processed in real-time RAM and never recorded elsewhere.
  • Storage/Files: To save your finished PDFs to your gallery or local folder.
  • Biometrics: If you enable App Lock, your fingerprint/FaceID data is handled by the OS; the App never sees your biometric data.

05. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with advertisers. Because we do not store your data, there is no database for us to leak or sell. The only third parties involved are Google/Apple for payment processing and optional crash reporting (Sentry/Firebase).
